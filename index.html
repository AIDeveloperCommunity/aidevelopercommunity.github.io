We are here to make a difference

We are a group of students who have embarked on a mission to build an AI-Powered environment. We are members of an open community and we organise various events to propagate knowledge of AI among developers.

What we do

We organise meetups, tech discussions, and different coding programmes on Artificial Intelligence and its related fields. We bring amazing people together to design and develop AI powered applications and share our work for the benefit of all.

Our Mission

To foster interactions among students and professionals interested in AI. To create awareness about AI and its applications. To encourage individuals to take an interest in open source society and extend learning among peers.
